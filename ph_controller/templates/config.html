Dosing Parameters

Higher → large doses even for small errors (fast but risks overshoot).
Lower → dose ramps up slowly, gentle correction.
0 → always doses the full max volume regardless of error.

Demo Mode simulation

Useful for demonstrating pump alternation without live hardware.
